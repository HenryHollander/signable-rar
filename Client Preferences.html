What do these terms mean again?

We'll help you choose the best cover

No more mumbo-jumbo. Let's explain these four important insurance terms.

💳
Monthly premium

This is what you will pay every month for your insurance.

Ⓡ
Excess

This is an agreed amount you will pay in when you have a claim.

📄
Essential cover

This is crucial to have. For example, it would cover the cost of repairs to your vehicle in case of an incident.

📋
Convenience cover

This is the nice to haves. For example, it would provide accommodation if your vehicle breaks down when you're travelling.
